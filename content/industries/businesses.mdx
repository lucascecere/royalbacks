Your team is the most visible part of your brand. Embroidered uniforms and branded apparel signal that your business is serious — about quality, about presentation, about lasting more than one season.

We work with South Shore businesses of all sizes. The contractor with 8 field technicians who need embroidered work shirts. The restaurant group that wants consistent staff polos across three locations. The real estate office that wants agents to look polished at open houses.

## What We Embroider for Businesses

**Polo shirts**: The workhouse of business embroidery. Left-chest logo, professional weight fabric, available in every color. Starting from 12 pieces.

**Work shirts and button-downs**: Oxford shirts for office environments, work shirts for field crews. Your logo, your name on the back, or both.

**Hoodies and crewnecks**: Company sweatshirts for team outings, client gifts, or cold-weather uniforms.

**Jackets and vests**: Softshell, fleece, and insulated jackets for contractors, outdoor staff, and anyone who works outside in a Massachusetts winter.

**Hats**: Company hats for staff, clients, and trade shows.

## The Process

1. Fill out our quote form — 3 minutes
2. We reply within one business day with a quote
3. Send us your logo (AI or high-res PDF preferred)
4. Approve the digital proof
5. Production: 10–14 business days
6. Delivered to your door or available for Milton pickup

Simple. Professional. Done right.
