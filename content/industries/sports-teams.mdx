Sports teams need gear that can take a season. Hats that survive a dugout all summer. Polos that look sharp at an awards banquet. Practice shirts that hold up through twice-a-week ice sessions.

We've been doing team embroidery on the South Shore since 2017, starting with local hockey associations and expanding from there. If your league or club needs matching gear, here's what we do.

## What We Embroider for Teams

**Hats**: Structured 6-panel caps, unstructured dad hats, beanies for winter sports. Left-chest or front panel logos, optional side hit.

**Polos**: Team polo shirts in bulk — coaching staff uniforms, booster club shirts, end-of-season appreciation gifts.

**Practice gear**: Embroidered crewnecks and hoodies for off-ice, post-practice, and travel days.

**Jackets**: Softshell and fleece for coaching staff and managers. These last for years and look professional.

## Volume Pricing

We break pricing at 6, 12, 24, and 48 pieces. The more you order, the lower the per-piece cost. Most teams find that 24 pieces gets them to a price point that makes sense.

## Our Process for Team Orders

1. You fill out a quote form or email us with your item type, quantity, and a description of your logo
2. We send a quote within 24 hours
3. You approve the quote and send us your artwork (AI, PDF, or high-res PNG)
4. We digitize the design (one-time fee, $35–$75 depending on complexity)
5. You approve the digital proof
6. Production runs — 10–14 business days for standard orders
7. Gear ships to your address or is available for Milton pickup
