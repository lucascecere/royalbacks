Hockey gear is where Royal Backs started. Dylan McDougall grew up playing hockey, and the first custom order we ever did was for a youth hockey association on the South Shore. That was 2017. We've been doing hockey gear every season since.

## Why Hockey Teams Choose Royal Backs

We're not a general print shop that also does embroidery. Embroidery is what we do, and hockey teams have been a core part of our business since day one. We understand the seasonal timeline, the rush before tryouts, and what matters to association directors who are managing a $50,000 equipment budget and just need the gear to show up right and on time.

## What Hockey Teams Order

**Association and club hats**: The most common order. Structured 6-panel caps with the association logo on the front. Parents want them. Coaches wear them. They're everywhere in hockey rinks across the South Shore.

**Coaching staff polos**: Left-chest logo, professional appearance for game days and parent meetings.

**Volunteer and board gear**: Associations need their board members and volunteers to look organized. Embroidered polos or crewnecks with the association name.

**Travel team gear**: Travel programs often want gear that looks different from the house league — embroidered snapbacks or fitted caps with a distinct travel program logo.

**Beanies and cold-weather gear**: For the association families spending every Saturday morning in a cold rink.
